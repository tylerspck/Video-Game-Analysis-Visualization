Game Sales by Publisher

When looking at the dataset, we were curious to see if there was a correlation between the number of games a publisher released and the total sales. We were able to make a scatter plot a preform a linear regression to make a line of best fit.

From the above graph, you can see that the line has a strong fit with an r-square of 0.808. This show a very strong linear fit. There is only one outlier standing out. That is the lone point that relates to 700 on the x-axis. This lone point represents Nintendo. They released 700 games, but have total sales of 1.7 billion. What boasts this number is the Wii Sports game sales, that people really did not buy. But this number shows the impressive amount of consols that Nintendo was able to sell.
