Top Publishers by Region

Here we took a look at the top 10 Video Game Publisher and their overall sales divided by region. With this graph we can see that Nintendo blows away the next closest Publisher. Nintendo had 1.786 billion dollars in game sales where Electronic Arts had 1.171 billion in sales. From this you can see again that North America has the majority of the sales with Europe in second for the most part.

We took it one step further with the publishers and broke down what types of games they make the most. We were curious if certain publishers stuck to one genre of game or had a more diverse production. With the pie charts above, you can see that the majority of these publishere stick to one type of game mostly. But then you have companies like Nintendo that with mix it up with a lot of variaty.
