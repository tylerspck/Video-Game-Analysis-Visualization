Genre Vs. Regional Sales

For this section, we took a deeper look at what genres did the best in what regions. We were able to sort the game information into the Genre and then look at the sales for all of those specifics genres. From this graph you can see that the overall best selling genre in 3 out of the 4 regions was the Action Genre.

The only region that Action did not have the top sales was in Japan, where Role-Playing games had the best overall sales performance. This is also the only genre that had another region that North America topping the sales.
