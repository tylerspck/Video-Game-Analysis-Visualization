Summary: Video Game Sales Analysis

TThe purpose of this project was to take a deeper looking into the Sales of Video Games. This analysis was able to be completed with a data set that was found online that contained a list of games, the platform, and the regional sales. The regions were broken up into North American sales, European Sales, Japanese Sales and the Rest of the World.

We were able to use Pandas with Matplotlib to visulize different ways to look at the information from the dataset. Some of the factors we decided to focus on were the Sales vs Publishers, Sales per Platform, Most sucessfil platform, The best selling Genre, The best selling genre for each region and the Best selling Publishers. This site provides the sorce data dn visualizations that were created as part of this analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations
