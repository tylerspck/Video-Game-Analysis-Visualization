Top Selling Platforms by Region

For the chart above, we decided to take a look at the different regions and the platforms that had their sales top in those regions. By the way we sorted the data, each platform will only appear in the region that sold the most for that consol.

Where there was a lot of varity for North America and Japan, Europe only had two platforms were they had the most game sales and the Rest of the World did not top the list for any platform. In Europe, only had top sales for the PS4 and PC games. The PC games sales makes sense due to the fact that in North America there seems to be a decline in computer sales due to mobile devices.
